# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing pages
- Service pages
- Location pages
- Floor plan pages
- Gallery pages
- Blog, FAQ, glossary, guides, and reference-library content
- Crawlability, metadata, structured data, internal linking, AI crawler readiness, and source-visible accessibility/performance SEO signals

## Out of scope
- Authenticated application areas (none identified in this artifact)
- Admin and dev-only routes such as `app/%5F_dev/**`
- `/api/**`

## Target audience
- Homebuyers and landowners researching custom homes in Scottsdale, Rio Verde, Phoenix, and nearby Arizona markets
- Prospects comparing custom-home, spec-home, floor-plan, financing, and build-on-your-lot options
- Research-phase visitors looking for permitting, zoning, land, financing, FAQ, glossary, and guide content

## Primary keywords
- custom home builder scottsdale
- custom home builder phoenix
- custom homes rio verde
- spec homes arizona
- build on your lot arizona
- arizona floor plans
- scottsdale custom homes

## Rendering model
- Next.js App Router with static export (`output: "export"`) for public routes
- Public pages are statically rendered to HTML and should be visible to search, social, and AI crawlers without JavaScript
